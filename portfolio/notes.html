Description:

There are many demands on todays busniness owner. For example, the owner of a business needs to track; pricing, competitiveness, expansion, marketing, inventory, financing and salaries. This note taker application will aid a business owner in all of these task. The application uses and express backend to save and retrieve note data from a JSON file.
